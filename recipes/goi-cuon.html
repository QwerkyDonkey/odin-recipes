Goi Cuon

Ingredients For Spring Roll

  • Pork belly
  • Shrimp
  • Vermicelli Noodles
  • Rice paper
  • Other fillings (lettuce, carrot, cucumber, mint)

Ingredients For Hoison Dipping Sauce

  • Hoison sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peanut butter
  • Water

Steps

  1. Lay the rice paper on a flat plate. Place lettuce, mint, carrots, cucumbers, noodles, and pork belly on one end of the rice paper.
  2. Roll it halfway. Make sure to roll it as tight as possible, so it's easier to dip and eat. Place 3-4 shrimp right in front of your roll. This will allow for the shrimp to appear neatly at the end.
  3. Roll over the shrimp as tightly as possible. Tuck in the sides of the roll before completing your last roll.
