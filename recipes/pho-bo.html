Pho Bo

Ingredients For Broth

  • 1/2 kg chopped beef or pork bones
  • 150g beef cuts (cheek)
  • 1.5L cold water
  • 20g sliced root ginger
  • 4-5 cloves garlic
  • 3 peeled shallots then cut in half
  • 2- 3 star anise
  • 3 small pieces of cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 tablespoons of fish sauce

Ingredients For Serving

  • 300g sliced beef steak
  • 200g fine rice noodles (Bánh Phở)
  • 2 tablespoons of sliced spring onion
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped small red chilies
  • 100g beansprouts
  • 120g sweet basil, peppermint and coriander leaves
  • 3-4 slices of lemon
